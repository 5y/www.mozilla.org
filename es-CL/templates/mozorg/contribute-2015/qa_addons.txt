Thank you for your interest in Add-ons. Addons.mozilla.org, commonly known as "AMO", is Mozilla's official site for add-ons. Add-ons let you personalize your browsing experience on Firefox so you can make it your own. Contribute to user freedom by keeping Firefox the most customizable browser available.

There are many ways to contribute to this project, and every skill level is welcome. Your efforts matter!

Please check our Wiki page for the most up-to-date information on contribution opportunities: https://wiki.mozilla.org/Marketplace/Contributing/Addons

If you have any questions, please feel free to email us!
